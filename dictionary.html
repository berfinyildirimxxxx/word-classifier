Type Descriptions

Type 1 — Common

Words that most people know and use regularly in everyday conversation. Basic, familiar words.

Type 2 — Intermediate

Words that educated people know but might not use every day. Slightly less common, but still widely understood.

Type 3 — Rare / Specialized

Words that only specialists, academics, or older generations would know. Uncommon in daily speech.
